Now that I have your attention,
Apologies for letting you down,
Apologies for the things I've done,
Apologies for not being around,
Apologies for being on the run,

Forgive me for hidin',

Dear friend,

I'm writing this from bottom of my heart.I feel ashamed of myself for not fulfilling my promise and I am profoundly sorry.

It had been a long time since our last conversation It had been a long time since we last talked, I remember promising you something which would have been very beneficial for both of us and in terms would have been very fruitful, we both agreed on it. BUT I failed myself in delivering my promise and in the process failed your very dream that could have been. I can understand if you are still angry for what I did and do not trust my words for that I only blame myself, you have every right to be enraged as I have not been a good friend.

I know you must loath me now, and I understand you don't want anything to do with me. My friend, I am writing to say that I am deeply sorry for hurting your feelings. I did the one thing that I swore never to do. It was stupid of me, and I know I disappointed you. Please, please, please forgive me. Our friendship means the world to me, and I wouldn't want to lose you. I know it took me a long time to reach you, I didn't have enough courage to reach you I was in a passive self-centered state and had no regards for my life I was in a bad shape when it all happened, I wanted to tell you all about it but I was too afraid to gather the courage to talk even now I am afraid. I had been very depressed for a year now all that I thought of doing all my ambitions , expectations, and failure to adjust to the lockdown regime hit very hard I used to consider myself a very brave, courageous, and courteous human being turns out I am not as brave I thought It was very difficult to eliminate the problem when the problem is within your lurking and slowly eating you from inside like a blood-sucking parasite but instead of blood it sucks your own consciousness and with that your self-confidence shatters and without you realizing you are no longer in control and the devil inside is eats away your will power.
